      Quick help
      • prefix search: default
        e.g., sig matches "SIGIR" as well as "signal"
      • exact word search: append dollar ($)
        e.g., graph$ does not match "graphics"
      • phrase search: connect by dot (.)
        e.g., inform.retriev.tech
      • boolean and: separate by space
        e.g., codd model
      • boolean or: connect by pipe (|)
        e.g., graph|network
      • boolean not: prepend minus (-)
        e.g., knuth -don
